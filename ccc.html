Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes dozens of delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles loaded with chocolate chips!

Steps

  • butter
  • vanilla
  • baking soda
  • water
  • salt
  • flour
  • chocolate chips
  • heath bars (mini)

Directions

  1. beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
  2. dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. put the heath bars (mini) in a small ziploc bag and crush with your hands
  4. stir in the flour, choclate chips, and crushed heath bars
  5. drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  6. bake until the edges are golden brown
