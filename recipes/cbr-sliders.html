Chicken Bacon Ranch Sliders

Description

These chicken bacon ranch sliders are a perfect finger food for game day.

The cooked chicken is flavored with ranch dressing and the top of the sliders are seasoned with ranch butter for a tasty appetizer that requires minimal prep.

Ingredients

  • 8 strips of bacon
  • 12 Hawaiian dinner rolls
  • 1 pound shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup prepared ranch dressing
  • 8 ounces of shredded colby-jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons of ranch seasoning

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. place bacon in large skillet, cook over medium-high heat for 10 minutes (or unitl evenly brown)
  3. drain bacon slices on paper towels
  4. when cool enough to handle, crumble bacon
  5. with serrated knife, cut slider rolls in half horizontally
  6. place bottom halves of rolls onto rimmed baking tray
  7. add chicken and prepared ranch dressing to a bowl; stir to throughly to coat chicken
  8. spread chicken onto slider rolls
  9. top chicken mixture with cheese and crumbled bacon
  10. place top halves on top of filling
  11. melt butter in a bowl with microwave on high for 30 seconds
  12. stir ranch seasoning into butter
  13. with pastry brush, brush tops of sliders with ranch butter -- cover rolls with foil
  14. bake into preheated oven for 20 minutes
  15. remove foil and bake for about 10 minutes until cheese is melted
