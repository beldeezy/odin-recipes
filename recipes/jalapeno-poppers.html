Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Quick & easy! Only 3 ingredients required: bacon, a block of Cheddar cheese and jalapeno peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package Cheddar cheese
  • 6 jalapeno peppers, seeded and halved
  • 12 slices of bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat the broiler.
  2. Cut cheddar cheese into 12 slices long enough to fit inside the jalapeno halves.
  3. Insert cheese slices into the halves.
  4. Wrap the jalapeno halves with the bacon slices. (Secure with toothpick, if necessary)
  5. Broil 5 to 10 minutes, or until bacon is evenly brown.
